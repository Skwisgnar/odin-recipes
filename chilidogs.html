Chilidogs

Description

This is a very cheap and easy recipe only requiring three ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Hotdog buns
  • Bar S Classic Franks
  • Can of Hormel brand Chili with Beans

Cooking steps

  1. Boil hotdogs (4 to 5 minutes total)
  2. While hotdogs are boiling, begin heating chili on stove until warm or pour into microwave safe dish and microwave for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes
  3. Once hotdogs are finished boiling, place in buns and top with warm chili and enjoy!
