Teryaki Chicken

Description

Another cheap and easy recipe only requiring four ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Instant white rice
  • Bag of steamable frozen vegetables
  • Bottle of Kikkoman teryaki glaze

Cooking steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts into bite sized cubes and cook on stove in a pan over medium-low heat with a little olive oil, once fully cooked pour in the glaze and stir well
  2. Cook desired amount of instant rice in microwave according to the brand's instructions
  3. Steam vegetables according to the brand's instructions
  4. After all ingredients are ready to go, make your bowl or plate however you desire and enjoy!
